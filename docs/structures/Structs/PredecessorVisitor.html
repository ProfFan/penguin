PredecessorVisitor

public struct PredecessorVisitor<Graph: IncidenceGraph>: DFSVisitor
where Graph.VertexId: IdIndexable

A DFSVisitor that records the parents of every discovered vertex.

PredecessorVisitor allows capturing a representation of the DFS tree, as this is often a useful output of a DFS traversal within other graph algorithms.
